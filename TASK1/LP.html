ABOUT

BHARAT-BHASHA is a non-profit initiative taken up by many collaboratives within Bharat with varying linguistic backgrounds to spread,learn and improve the regional languages of Bharat.

The divine and beautiful bird of language, with its magnificent and splendid wings spread out all around, flies far over the entire nation of India, drawing everyone's attention upward. Language functions as a tool for heart-to-heart communion in addition to being a means of communication for people because it is filled with aesthetic beauty and poetic words.

LANGUAGES OFFERED

HINDI

It's best to start with the language most expanded upon;described as a standardised and Sanskritised register of the Hindustani language, which itself is based primarily on the Khariboli dialect of Delhi and neighbouring areas of North India.

Take a trip in hindi today.

BANGLA

An Indo-Aryan language native to the Bengal region of South Asia;Considered the sweetest language in the world.
(Note:Indian Bengali)

Take a trip in Bengali today.

TAMIL

A classical Dravidian language natively spoken by the Tamil people of South Asia. Tamil is an official language of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu, the sovereign nations of Sri Lanka and Singapore, and the Union Territory of Puducherry.

Take a trip in Tamil today.
